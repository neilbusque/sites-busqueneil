Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

This privacy policy explains how Neil Busque ("I", "me", or "my") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit sites.busqueneil.com or submit the quote form.

What I collect

When you fill out the quote form, I collect: your name, business name, email address, phone number (if provided), current website URL (if provided), the answers you give to the form's questions, and any notes you add. I also collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) that any web host collects by default.

Why I collect it

  • To reply to your inquiry and schedule a call.
  • To prepare a quote for the work you're asking about.
  • To follow up after the call if we don't immediately move forward.

Who I share it with

I don't sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes. The form is processed by Netlify Forms (my hosting provider). Email correspondence is sent and stored using Google Workspace. Both companies have their own privacy practices.

How long I keep it

I keep form submissions and email correspondence indefinitely so I can pick up the conversation later if needed. You can ask me to delete it at any time by emailing busqueneil@gmail.com.

Cookies

This site does not use tracking cookies. There is no Meta Pixel, Google Analytics, or third-party advertising tracker on this page.

Your rights

You can ask me what information I have about you, ask me to correct it, or ask me to delete it. Email busqueneil@gmail.com and I'll respond within a few business days.
